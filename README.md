Complete **Rework** of how the **Vanilla Shield** works. Giving it new **Mechanics/Abilities** aswell as **Customizability** and more **Advancements**.

The shield mod is **Meant to be PvE** mostly, but can be configured fully to make it easier on the **PvP side**. _(Less punishing)_

There is **Resourcepack support** for this mod! [**Enhanced Shield!**](https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/enhanced-shield!/version/1.0) Puts the shield on the side to not clutter the screen so much!

## Mechanics
Being a **Rework** of course it expands the **Mechanics of the Shield** offering it more advantages for **Defending** as well as **turning the shield into a Offensive** Item **becoming a Weapon that can become deadly in certain scenarios**.

<details>
<summary>All Mechanics/Abilities Extended Info.</summary>

### Parry
**Enemies** can now be **Parried** within a small Window. **To execute** it you must **pull up your shield at the exact time** someone is about to **hit you**.
Dealing **small knockback** to the **attacker** and **Stun**. _(wether player or mob)_

**[Timing can be Extended with Parryful Enchantment]**

![Shield Parry](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/2rRvPapc/images/b48f3d84539711c496eed562deadbc3c2bd9aa5c.gif)

### Arrow Deflect
You're able to **Deflect Arrows**/**Projectiles** by **Doing a Parry** at the right time. Making their **Attack reroute** to the **direction where you're looking** _(Even fireballs can be deflected)_

**[Damage attribute can be Increased with Deflector Enchantment]**

![Arrow Deflect](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/2rRvPapc/images/f36bf94085ad740beaec4f9972ff062428cc74f6.gif)

### Bash
By **holding up Block** and **Pressing Attack** you're able to do a **Bash** going **forward 4 blocks** and **Causing Damage** AND **Stun** to the _Enemies_ aswell as **Throwing them away to control the distance.**

**[Damage can be Increased with Shield Density Enchantment]**

![Shield Bash](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/2rRvPapc/images/74a03aa15b9313ecc23ce3b79532d1870567e6e1.gif)

### Slam
**While in Air atleast 3 Blocks** above the ground you're able to perform a **Slam** causing a **AoE** of **5 blocks** around you.

**[Damage can be Increased with Shield Density Enchantment]**

![Slam](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/2rRvPapc/images/ef307fe7a20b3e2befca51e34d12c741e70fc421.gif)

### Shield Throw [Needs Enchant]
If **Loyalty enchant** is _present_ on the **shield** you can **Throw your shield** with Middle mouse Dealing damage and Stun. _(Keybind can be replaced)_

**[Piercing can be added to go through Multiple Entities, however it doesn't do anything on it's own without Loyalty]**

![Shield Throw](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/2rRvPapc/images/e4efa6a1cccc9a5ed728f70d9a55e6e272810ffb.gif)

## Enchantments
The mod features new Enchantments to Turn it officially into a Weapon that can Harm even the strongest Foes that may come across. From Cooldowns to Offense ability adaptions.

<details>
<summary>Custom Enchantments Info</summary>

### Shield Density I / II / III
**Slam**, **Shield Throw** and **Bash** deal more damage. maximum is 30% (each level adds 10%+)

### Parryful I / II / III 
**Increases the duration** of your **active parry** Maximum 0.6s+ (each level adds 0.2s+)

### Deflector I / II 
Adds **more damage to arrow deflects** Maximum 30% (each level adds 15%)

### Parry Doctorine I / II - NOT AVAILABLE BEYOND 1.0.5 (1.0.61+ have this removed)
When **parrying an attack** or doing a **Arrow Deflect** it will **mend the Shield 3%**. Maximum 6% (repairs the shield basically)

### Slam Foam I / II
When **Slamming down** Increases the **fall damage protection** while executing slam Maximum 50% (25% per level)

### Masterine I / II
**Reduces the cooldown** of the Abilities Maximum 40% (each level adds 20%)

### Active Armor I / II / III
**Reduces incoming damage** by 4% per level while **held in the off-hand**.

## Other Mod Features


<details>
<summary>Shield System Mode/s</summary>

The Shield system now has three modes the User/Admin of a server can pick out.

**Default** - When a parry is hit, bash, slam, shield throw, arrow deflect; Always results in a stun.

**Low HP** - If an enemy is below 25% HP (Can be configured) they'll only then be stunned.

**Gauge** - If an enemy gets stunned 3 (Can be configured) times, only then they'll be stunned.

What deals Gauge/Stun also changes how these systems work.

- Stun Snap - If in a stunned state, and got hit 2 (Can be Configured) times, you'll break out of the stun (Enabled by default)
- When stunned (as a player) Your inventory becomes Disabled, making you completely vulnerable
- When a Parry gets hit by an Axe, the parry Breaks and disables the Shield fully. (Balanced update)

</details>

<details>
<summary>Mod Blacklist</summary>

By **default** the mod will not react.

_(such as a Bug where if the shield is in Offhand and you're aiming down sights and with a gun and shooting, the shield will Bash forward. Blacklist fixes that.)_

If an item is present in the **Main hand** from the **Mod ID** that's included in the "Compatability" category it will not react. Meaning it **disables the shield** for the meantime.

The category add is slightly broken for now (will get fixed soon as I add the Item Blacklist)

</details>

<details>
<summary>Quality of Life Updates</summary>

It wouldn't be a good Rework if the Stupid issues weren't removed from the vanilla game such as Animation Shield delay.

- By default, the 5 tick delay animation is now Completely removed, making the shield instant as soon as the server obtains the ping.
- The Shield can now be enchanted directly through the Enchantment Table.
- The Shield supports minecraft enchants such as "Loyalty" (Loyalty is needed for Shield Throw to work, if it doesn't exist it will not Throw itself at all.) and Piercing (basically allows the Shield throw to go through Multiple entities upon being thrown.
- Advancements Meant to instruct the player on what the Shield can do.
- Swift Sneak now increases your movement speed when strafing and blocking with a shield.
- Shield Throw now upon return Hits any targets dealing damage while traveling back to the user
